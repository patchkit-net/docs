Indie & Student Plans

We offer forever free accounts for the indie developers and students, on which you can get:

  • All standard features
  • 500 GiB of monthly data transfer
  • Custom Launcher - ability to customize it at will
  • Unlimited number of applications

I consider myself an Indie developer. How can I apply?

We require from you two things for validation:

  • A working website.
  • Information about us somewhere on it (details below)
  • A PatchKit account (you can set it up for free)

To help us grow, we ask to put a piece of information about PatchKit somewhere on your project’s website with a link to our landing page: https://patchkit.net/. Please download this package and use one of these images.

When done, please navigate to Switch Plan page, click on Indie / Student tab and fill in the form as an indie developer. Don’t forget to provide your valid website address!

I am a student. How can I apply?

Applying as a student requires an e-mail address in your school’s or university’s domain. There are usually *.edu domains. When you have one, set up a PatchKit account using that domain, then navigate to Switch Plan page, click on the Indie / Student tab and fill in the form as a student.

FAQ

Is 500 GiB of data transfer enough for my game?

In most cases, yes! Due to our observations, this seems to be a reasonable threshold. If you happen to get over it, it could mean that this is a good moment where you can start making good money on your game!

What happens if I exceed 500 GiB?

If this happens occasionally, then we’re cool with it. We are happy to see projects grow with the help of PatchKit! If you stay over 500 GiB for a good amount of time, we will contact you to negotiate an exclusive deal.

Can I sell my game with PatchKit?

You can! Please contact us for more information.

How long will it take to get the approval?

It’s usually 1-3 days.
